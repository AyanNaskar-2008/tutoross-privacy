Delete Your TutorOS Account

You can delete your TutorOS account directly from within the app. No need to contact us.

How to delete your account in the app

  • Open TutorOS
  • Tap your profile picture (top right)
  • Scroll down and tap Delete Account
  • Confirm deletion in the dialog

What gets deleted

  • Your account and login credentials
  • All batches, student records, fee data (teacher accounts)
  • All messages and notifications
  • Your profile information and avatar

What is not automatically deleted

  • Notes uploaded to your Google Drive — these must be deleted manually from your Google Drive account in the TutorOS Notes folder

Data retention

All server data is permanently deleted immediately upon account deletion. There is no retention period.
